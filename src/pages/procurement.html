BAC Overview

The Bids and Awards Committee (BAC) manages procurement activities for the Schools Division Office. This hub provides access to current opportunities, results, and procurement documents.

All notices are subject to official posting requirements. Please monitor PhilGEPS and the Division channels for updates.
