How to Apply

The general process for division-facilitated scholarship applications.

1
Check Eligibility
Review requirements for your target scholarship. Confirm employment status, rank, and other qualifications with HRMD.
2
Prepare Documents
Gather all required documents (see below). Ensure certified true copies are secured from the issuing office.
3
Submit to SDO
Submit your complete application packet to the Human Resource Management Division of the SDO.
4
SDO Endorsement
The Division reviews and endorses qualified applicants to the scholarship-granting body on your behalf.
5
Awarding
Scholars are notified by the granting body. Recipients sign a contract and service obligation agreement.

General Requirements

Most programs require the documents below. Specific scholarships may have additional requirements.

📋 Personal Documents
  • Fully accomplished application form
  • Personal Data Sheet (CS Form 212)
  • Two (2) government-issued IDs (photocopies)
  • 2×2 recent ID photo (white background)
  • PSA-issued Birth Certificate
🏫 Employment Documents
  • Certificate of Employment
  • Service Record (latest, certified)
  • Copy of latest appointment
  • Last 2 performance ratings (at least Very Satisfactory)
  • Division Clearance
🎓 Academic Documents
  • Official Transcript of Records (TOR)
  • Diploma / Certificate of Graduation
  • Proof of enrollment or admission letter
  • Study plan or proposed program of study
  • Endorsement letter from School Head / SDS
