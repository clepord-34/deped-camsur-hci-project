Bidding Process Guide

Follow the steps below to submit a compliant bid.

  1. Review the ITB

    Download the invitation to bid and review eligibility requirements.

  2. Attend the pre-bid conference

    Join the pre-bid meeting for clarifications and updates.

  3. Prepare bid documents

    Complete the forms, price schedules, and required attachments.

  4. Submit on time

    Deliver sealed bids before the stated deadline at the BAC Secretariat.
