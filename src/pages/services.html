First Time Using These Systems?

Follow these four steps to get started with any DepEd online system.

🪪
Verify Your Account
Ensure your employee ID and DepEd email are registered in the HRIS before accessing other systems.
🔑
Get Your Credentials
Contact HR or the ICT Unit to receive your username and initial system password.
🌐
Use an Updated Browser
Use Google Chrome or Mozilla Firefox with a stable internet connection. Avoid Internet Explorer.
🎫
Need Help? File a Ticket
Visit the ICT Helpdesk to submit a support request or find quick fix guides.
