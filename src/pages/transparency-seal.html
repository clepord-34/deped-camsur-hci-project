Transparency Seal Overview

The Transparency Seal is a national initiative that requires government agencies to publish key documents for public access. The Schools Division Office posts materials that show how programs are planned, implemented, and evaluated.

The items below are organized by category for quick reference. Updates are posted regularly to keep stakeholders informed.

  • Annual reports and performance highlights

Requesting Records

If a file is not yet listed, you may request it from the Division.

  1. Identify the document

    Note the title, year, and office where the document is usually filed.

  2. Send a formal request

    Email the Records Unit or visit the office with a written request.

  3. Receive the response

    The office will acknowledge the request and provide the available copy.
