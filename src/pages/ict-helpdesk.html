Common Issues & Quick Fixes

Try these steps before submitting a ticket — most issues resolve in minutes.

📧
Can't Access DepEd Email
  • Go to mail.deped.gov.ph
  • Use firstname.lastname@deped.gov.ph format
  • Use "Forgot Password" or contact ICT for reset
  • Clear browser cache and cookies, then retry
🌐
School Internet Issues
  • Restart the router/modem (wait 30 seconds)
  • Check if other devices are also affected
  • Verify the school's connection subscription status
  • Report prolonged outages to the ICT Unit
🖥️
LIS / HRIS System Access
  • Ensure you're using an updated browser (Chrome/Firefox)
  • Try a different network (mobile data, etc.)
  • Contact your School HRIS Coordinator first
  • Email ICT for account reactivation
📱
DepEd Commons / Google Apps
  • Install the latest version of the app
  • Log in using your DepEd Google account only
  • Clear app cache or re-install the app
  • Submit a ticket for account creation issues
🖨️
Printer / Hardware Issues
  • Check all physical connections and power
  • Reinstall or update printer drivers
  • Restart the print spooler service (Windows)
  • Request an on-site visit for hardware faults
🔐
Password & Account Issues
  • Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page
  • Check your registered email for a reset link
  • Contact HR for HRIS-related account problems
  • Contact ICT for DepEd email account recovery

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get an official DepEd email address? ▾
All regular DepEd employees are entitled to an official email (firstname.lastname@deped.gov.ph). Contact the ICT Unit via email or walk-in and bring a copy of your appointment paper and a valid ID. Processing typically takes 1–2 working days.
What systems does the ICT Unit support? ▾
We support: LIS, HRIS, DepEd Email (Google Workspace), DepEd Commons, EBEIS, iGo Payroll, and Phil-IRI Online. For issues with national DepEd systems, we coordinate with DepEd Central ICT on your behalf.
How long does a support ticket take to resolve? ▾
Simple issues (password resets, account unlocks) are usually resolved within 24 hours. Complex issues or on-site visits may take 3–5 working days depending on location and staff availability.
Can the ICT Unit visit our school for on-site support? ▾
Yes. On-site visits are scheduled based on priority and staff availability. Submit a ticket clearly describing the issue, your school name, and a contact person. The ICT Unit will coordinate a schedule with you.
How do I report a cybersecurity or phishing concern? ▾
Immediately report suspicious emails, phishing attempts, malware, or unauthorized access to ict.camsur@deped.gov.ph. Do not click suspicious links. Disconnect the affected device from the network if possible and do not power it off before reporting.
